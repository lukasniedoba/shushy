# Shushy Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-07

Shushy ("the app") is designed to help parents and caregivers play calming sounds for babies.

## Data Collection

Shushy does not collect personal data on external servers.

Shushy does not use analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Data Stored on Device

The app stores limited settings locally on your device using iOS storage:

- Last played sound
- Sleep timer preference
- Fade-out preference
- Favorite sounds

This local data is used only to provide app functionality and is not shared with third parties.

## Tracking

Shushy does not track users across apps or websites.

## Third-Party Services

Shushy uses Apple system frameworks for core app functionality, including audio playback, Siri Shortcuts, and AirPlay.

## Children and Family Use

Shushy is intended for use by parents and caregivers. The app does not include user accounts, chat, social feeds, or user-generated content.
